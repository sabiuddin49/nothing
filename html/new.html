About Us

Government Polytechnic is a leading diploma college offering a wide range of technical programs. Established in 1985, we have been at the forefront of technical education, providing students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the industry.

Our mission is to empower students with practical and theoretical knowledge, fostering innovation and creativity. We believe in shaping not just engineers, but future leaders.

Our Diploma Programs

We offer a variety of diploma programs designed to meet the demands of the ever-evolving technical industry. Our programs include:

  • Diploma in Mechanical Engineering
  • Diploma in Electrical Engineering
  • Diploma in Civil Engineering
  • Diploma in Computer Science and Engineering
  • Diploma in Electronics and Communication Engineering

Our Facilities

At Government Polytechnic, we provide state-of-the-art facilities to ensure our students have the best learning experience. Our campus includes:
